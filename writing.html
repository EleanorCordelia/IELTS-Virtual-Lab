Tips & Tricks

General Writing Task 1

You recently attended an event organized by a local community group, but you were dissatisfied with certain aspects of the event. Write a letter to the event organizer.

In your letter:

  • Describe the event and why you attended.
  • Mention the issues that were disappointing.
  • Suggest ways the event could be improved in the future.

You do NOT need to write any addresses. Begin your letter as follows:

Dear Sir / Madam,

Write at least 150 words. You should spend about 20 minutes on this task.
